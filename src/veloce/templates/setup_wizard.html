Telegram Login

Sign in with your Telegram account to start listening for messages.

How to log in
  1. Enter your phone number in international format (example: +60123456789).
  2. Click Start Telegram login to receive a verification code.
  3. Enter the code and click Verify code.
  4. If prompted, enter your Telegram 2FA password and verify again.

Google Login

Sign in with your Google account to enable calendar scheduling.

Prerequisites
  1. Set ENABLE_GOOGLE_SYNC=true in your .env file.
  2. Set GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID and GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET in .env.
  3. Add this redirect URI in Google Cloud Console: http://127.0.0.1:8765/google/oauth/callback
  4. Click Sign in with Google below to complete the OAuth flow.
